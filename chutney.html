Description: Homemade chutney powder in Udupi style is a versatile and flavorful condiment, perfect for adding a spicy kick to South Indian meals. It typically includes roasted lentils (like urad dal), dried red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida, finely ground to a coarse powder. This traditional Udupi recipe balances heat and tanginess, enhancing dishes like dosas and idlis with its robust flavor profile. Making it at home ensures freshness and allows for adjusting spice levels according to personal taste preferences, making Udupi-style chutney powder a kitchen essential for spice lovers.
